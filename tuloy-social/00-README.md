# Tuloy — Social Media Kit

Your starter kit for running **Tuloy's** Facebook + Instagram. Tuloy is the commission-free direct-booking tool for **San Juan, La Union accommodation operators** — it turns a Facebook page into a live bookable calendar with GCash deposits, kills double-bookings, and replaces the Messenger-and-spreadsheet chaos. **Goal: awareness with operators.**

## What's inside

1. **01-strategy.md** — Audience (operators), core message, brand voice, 5 content pillars, cadence, hashtags, 90-day growth plan.
2. **02-content-calendar.md** — 4 weeks of ready-to-post FB + IG captions written for operators. Swap in real links/prices/names where you see [brackets].
3. **graphics/** — 3 sample posts (1080×1080 PNG, ready to upload) + editable SVG source:
   - `01-welcome.png` — intro / pinned post ("Your Facebook page can take bookings now")
   - `02-pain.png` — relatable pain post ("POV: you just double-booked Room 2")
   - `03-cta.png` — call to action ("Stop running bookings on Messenger")

## How to use it

Post `01-welcome.png` first and pin it. Then follow the Week 1 calendar. Replace every [bracket] with a real detail, and keep claims accurate to the product (no commission ever · deposits go straight to the operator's own GCash · free during pilot · don't promise the tourist marketplace yet).

## Keep it accurate

Pricing after pilot is room-gated: Solo ≤4 rms ₱990 · Pro ≤15 ₱2,500 · Business ≈₱15–25k. Confirm any feature or price with the team before publishing.
